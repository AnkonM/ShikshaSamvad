Empowering Students, One Conversation at a Time

ShikshaSamvad is an AI-powered ecosystem that proactively identifies students in distress, predicts dropout risks, and provides 24/7 anonymous, stigma-free counseling to foster mental well-being and academic success.

The Challenge

The Silent Struggle in Education

Today's students navigate a complex world of academic pressure, social isolation, and mental health challenges. The path to success is often lonely and overwhelming.

High Stress & Anxiety

1 in 3 students report symptoms of anxiety or depression, leading to burnout and poor performance.

Stigma & Underuse

Fear of judgment prevents students from using on-campus counseling, which is often reactive, not preventive.

Lack of Access

Rural campuses often lack professional resources, leaving students isolated and without 24/7 personalized support.

Our Solution

Introducing ShikshaSamvad

We've built a digital mental health ecosystem that combines AI-powered early risk detection with a 24/7, stigma-free counseling chatbot. We turn reactive support into proactive empowerment.

Predictive Analytics
Our AI-ML engine identifies at-risk students early, using behavioral KPIs and LMS data to quantify dropout risk.
Anonymous & Empathetic Support
A 24/7 multilingual NLP chatbot provides a safe space for students to share feelings and receive instant support.
Actionable Institutional Insights
An anonymized wellness dashboard gives faculty insights to deploy interventions, while protecting student privacy.

Why ShikshaSamvad

A Smarter, More Empathetic Approach

AI Risk Assessment Engine

Uses machine learning and Monte Carlo simulations on factors like attendance, grades, and engagement to predict dropout risk and generate early alerts for faculty.

24/7 NLP Counseling Chatbot

A multilingual, anonymous chatbot offers empathetic conversation, mindfulness tips, and CBT-inspired exercises, escalating to human counselors only when necessary.

Privacy-First & Stigma-Free

Our unique angle ensures high adoption by providing a safe, anonymous space. Gamified wellness challenges further motivate consistent, healthy habits.

Impact & Benefits

Creating a Culture of Care

Our platform delivers tangible benefits for students, faculty, and the entire institution.

Improved Mental Health

Students experience reduced stress and anxiety due to timely and accessible 24/7 support from the anonymous chatbot. This continuous guidance leads to better emotional well-being.

Reduced Dropout Rates

Early intervention and consistent support prevent student disengagement and reduce dropout rates. The system can be scaled to other colleges, making it a viable model for broader adoption.

Higher Academic Performance

With improved mental wellness, students can focus better, which leads to enhanced academic performance and results. This also prepares them for the emotional pressures of the future workforce.

Data-Driven Insights

The system provides actionable, anonymized data insights to faculty and counselors, allowing for proactive, informed interventions. This data can even help inform national student wellness policies.

Reduced Stigma

The anonymous nature of the chatbot encourages more students to seek help, reducing the stigma associated with mental health support. It also fosters a culture of empathy and peer support.

Cost-Efficient & Scalable

The AI chatbot provides a cost-efficient and scalable counseling solution that reduces institutional expenses compared to traditional methods. It's a digital-first approach that also reduces paper usage.
